Frequently Asked Questions

What types of projects do you handle?

We handle a wide range of projects including corporate videos, commercials, documentaries, music videos, event coverage, brand content, and photography services. Our team adapts to projects of all scales and complexities.

What is your typical project timeline?

Project timelines vary based on complexity and scope. Simple corporate videos may take 2-3 weeks, while comprehensive commercial productions can take 4-8 weeks. We provide detailed timelines during our initial consultation.

Do you provide equipment and crew?

Yes, we provide all necessary equipment including cameras, lighting, audio equipment, and drones. Our experienced crew includes directors, cinematographers, sound engineers, and post-production specialists.

Can you work with our existing brand guidelines?

Absolutely! We work closely with your brand guidelines to ensure consistency across all visual content. Our creative team ensures your brand identity is reflected throughout the production process.

What's included in your post-production services?

Our post-production services include video editing, color grading, sound design, motion graphics, visual effects, and final delivery in multiple formats optimized for different platforms.
